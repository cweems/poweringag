Powering Agriculture 2013 Winners


In its inaugural round, more than 475 organizations from 80 countries applied to the Powering Agriculture Grand Challenge, offering market-based, clean energy innovations for the agricultural sphere in developing countries. Today we announce the 12 winners. Each of the winning organizations received awards of between $800,000 and $1.5 million, and we hope that this money will both serve to support great ideas from around the globe and offer farmers the solutions they need today and tomorrow.
