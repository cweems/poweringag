Powering Agriculture 2013 Winners


In its inaugural round, 30 finalists out of 475 applications from 80 countries were selected to compete in the final stage of the Powering Agriculture Grand Challenge. Of these 30 applicants, 12 were selected by the partners to travel to Washington DC for the Powering Agriculture Grand Challenge Winners Announcement and DevelopmentXChange on December 10-11, 2013. The winners received seed grants of between $800,000 and $1.5 million to continue developing their research and innovations. Learn more about their exciting work below!
